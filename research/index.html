Research

Genetics of biological invasions

I am interested on discovering the genetic basis of the evolutionary framework that underlies the successful life history of mosquitofish (Gambusia holbrooki), a highly aggressive invader in Mediterranean stream. In an ongoing project we are using RADtag sequencing of several populations of mosquitofish to study how the invasion process sculpts the genome of the species. I am also analyzing patterns of rapid selection on MHC loci in introduced populations of G. holbrooki using high-throughput sequencing data.
